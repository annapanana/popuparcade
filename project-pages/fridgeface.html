Fridge Face

December, 2016
Designer / Developer

Fridge Face is a friendly inventory management system for communal refrigerators. The app enables community members to view all fridge contents, filter food by categories, check expiration dates and receive announcements. Register to get started with Fridge Face for your community refrigerator, today!

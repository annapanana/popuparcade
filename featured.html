Fridge Face

Fridge Face is a friendly inventory management system for communal refrigerators. The app enables community members to view all fridge contents, filter food by categories, check expiration dates and receive announcements. Greetings from the Internet

Greetings from the Internet (gfti) is a web-based postcard making tool that allows users to search for an image, modify a greetings graphic and include a custom message. The aesthetic of gfti is an homage to Teich greetings postcards. Gfti uses the Flickr API to supply users with images and the Lob API to send postcards.

The Greenhouse

The Greenhouse is a board game about greenhouse gas and our atmosphere that I designed and produced in conjunction with the National Center of Atmospheric Research. The game seeks to teach 3rd graders - 5th graders about the influence that greenhouse gases have on our atmosphere.
